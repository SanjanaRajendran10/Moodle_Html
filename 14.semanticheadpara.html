FULL STACK DEVELOPMENT

Full stack development refers to the practice of working on both the front end and back end parts of a web application or software product. A full stack developer is proficient in multiple technologies and capable of handling all aspects of software development, including server, database, client, and front-end interfaces.

Front-end development: This involves working on the client-side of the application that users interact with directly.

Back-end development: This involves working on the server-side of the application that manages the database, server, and application logic.

UI/UX

UI (User Interface) design and UX (User Experience) design are two critical aspects of creating digital products, websites, or applications that provide an optimal experience for users.
